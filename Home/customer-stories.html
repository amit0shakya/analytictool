PVR

PVR is the largest and the most premium film and retail entertainment company of the nation.

It currently operates a cinema circuit comprising of 467 screens in 105 properties in 43 cities pan India.

  

Key Results:
  • Payment gateway debugging
  • Identified that forced-signup drove their users away from the app
  • Tracked down fraudulent and incent installs

 

The Challenge

For an entertainment company like PVR, it is critical to reach out to audience and attain maximum users.

Although the user base expanded, there was no considerable increase in the revenue and it continued to experience a nonlinear growth. PVR was not sure why it was losing audience while spending huge amounts of money.

The Solution

With rocQ’s behavioral mapping, PVR realized that there was a drastic decline in the number of users before and after their sign up event. They were losing potential users by forcing them to log in.

Moreover, by analyzing the user journey, PVR discovered that its users were dropping off at its payment gateway. PVR fixed the issue soon and now the users can make payment using the provider of their choice.

Furthermore, rocQ tracked down the fraudulent and incent installs for PVR which enabled it to spend the campaign budget intelligently into dependable sources.
