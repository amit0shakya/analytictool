FAQ

Tell us, what can we help you with & get answers to all your queries

  1. Can I use rocQ along with other analytical tools?

    Yes, our android & iOS libraries will work just fine if any other third-party services are also integrated with your app.

  2. How often will my data update?

    Typically your data will get updated every 24 hours. However, we provide real-time push reports (push open & receive) and campaign postback reports.

  3. What about privacy & security?

    We store your data anonymously. It is fully secured and encrypted with us.


rocQ is a mobile app analytics and user engagement platform that provides real-time actionable insights to businesses. We help app owners use data to manage user interaction with their apps and build a relationship with their user base.
