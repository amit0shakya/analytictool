Decoding the User Journey

If only the user landed on the home screen, selected a product, viewed the product details and purchased it right away without any distraction, it would be such a simple world for all of us. However your consumers including ourselves do not take a linear route to purchase. We get distracted by other products, phone calls, texts and this leads to an unpredictable user journey when using mobile applications.

We would be kidding ourselves if we still measured parameters like average time spent per screen. Most businesses are still in a transition phase when it comes to adapting towards a mobile based community of users. We can stumble on so many websites, who have still not rendered themselves mobile friendly. This transition also applies to the metrics we use to determine the quality of a user on a mobile application.

It has become amply clear to you by now that we all need valid metrics to measure user quality on mobile applications. It is highly vital that we track the real user journey on their mobile applications. We need to know the number of users who visit a particular screen; the drop-off rates at each particular screen; the calculation of how many people have actually gone on to purchasing a product after landing on a particular screen - these are actual metrics that are extremely meaningful.

rocQ cares deeply about what affects you and what keeps your brand or product manager awake at night. We understand that the transition to a mobile world is extremely difficult and you would rather focus on your core strengths than try building a team that can then invent ways to understand the consumer. We are doing that for you thereby enabling you to focus on your business. Leave the user tracking to us
