CAMPAIGN MANAGEMENT

Like any other app owner, you want to attain maximum installs. However, how do you know if the advertisers you are employing are getting you real or authentic users?

Every time a user installs your app, rocQ informs you and your referrer about the conversion. This transparency helps to create a better relationship between you and your advertisers. By considering an event as a benchmark, rocQ enables you to know if the users you just acquired are humans and not bots.

Benefits

  • Track fraudulent installs
  • Attribute credit to sources through post-back mechanism
  • Check users’ quality by setting an event as a benchmark

Example

You have a couple of sources who send you an audience. But when that audience becomes your user, how would you know if the user is authentic?

Set an event as when you generate a campaign. This way, when a user installs your app and performs that event, you’ll be notified about his/her legitimacy. It also enables you to know about your most dependable medium by keeping a track of the exact score of such quality installs.

Post Install Behavior

Wouldn’t it be awesome to have a tool to track your users’ journey across the app? You must be curious to know their most preferred actions.

As soon as a person downloads or opens your app, rocQ creates a real-time user profile for you. rocQ helps you track the events triggered across the app & analyze your users’ interaction with each screen individually. This way you can understand your users’ behavior throughout their lifetime & make the relevant changes for delivering a better service.

Benefits

  • Investigate which users spend most time on your app
  • Study the events that lead to drop-offs & conversions
  • Get highlights of most engaged and valuable users overtime

Example

Your users are adding items in their cart but what if they don’t end up purchasing them? How can you determine the possible problem?

rocQ enables you to track each event undertaken throughout your users’ journey across your app. This not only helps you determine what exactly is causing the hindrance, but also figures out areas of improvement.

Revenue

Your large user base consists of active as well as inactive users. It is essential to discern your inactive users from the active ones. In fact, determining if a user provides value back to your app is much more important than you may think it is. rocQ helps you selectively target those who are most likely to be interested in your content.

Discover your most beneficial users and figure out why they love your application. rocQ helps you understand what they are clicking on and who is bringing these users to you. This enables you to target future campaigns towards acquiring more relevant users.

Benefits

  • Determine the quality of your users
  • Hyper-target the profitable user base
  • Invest in traffic sources that convert

Example

You are getting users from two different mediums, A and B. Now, how would you figure out which one’s providing you with the most profitable users?

rocQ enables you to determine that Source A provides 100 users who contribute Rs. 100,000 to your revenue; while Source B provides 1000 users who contribute Rs. 50,000. Now, you know which medium is more dependable.

Segmentation

When it comes to delivering products or services, one size doesn’t fit all!

Offering content to relevant and irrelevant target users could very well annoy them, which might even lead them to uninstall your app. Rather, build loyal relationships with users by developing and offering them products and services they actually want.

Get a better understanding of your users’ activity based on their behavioral traits. rocQ groups similar users into smaller segments by factors such as geographical location, type and lifestyle of users, attitudes and behavior. This enables you to tailor your strategies to their interest.

Benefits

  • Plan strategies according to users’ behavioral traits
  • Re-engage with individual user segments
  • Focus on your most profitable users

 

Example

You have an electronics sale upcoming on your app in a couple of days. Why don’t you inform the user segment that has bought at least one electronic product in the past 6 months, about the new sale?

rocQ analyzes your data and based on the user behavior, extracts numerous patterns. This helps you offer relevant services to users that share a common characteristic.

Push Notification

Most of the mobile phone users keep their devices handy throughout the day. This means you can reach your users wherever they are instead of having to wait for them to access your app. With push notifications, you can follow your users wherever they go.

Push notifications offer numerous benefits in terms of engaging with the users and driving traffic to your app.  rocQ enables you to send personalized messages to users about relevant content and events. It is an intelligent approach to bring users back to the app.

Benefits

  • Personalized messages delivered to home screen
  • Track open rate of your push notifications
  • Use deep linking tactics to increase conversion

 

Example

Your users are eventually losing interest in your game. You target the user segment that hasn’t been active for past 2 months and send a push notification. What next?

rocQ determines the number of notifications sent, received and opened. The rate of notifications opened, directly affects your user activity.

In-app Messaging

Good communication requires attention. If your user has your attention already, why not cash in on this opportunity? How much will that ease your engagement with them!

You can directly communicate with your users individually, right inside your app with beautifully simple in-app messages. rocQ enables you to broadcast engaging notifications as soon as your users open your app. It is up to you to choose who receives your message by targeting segments of your users.

Benefits

  • Ad as an app experience
  • Present filtered content to your user base
  • Addresses the targeted audience at their point of need

Example

A section of your users haven’t missed the last 3 Airline flash sales. How about narrowing down your focus on them & increase the engagement and revenue?

rocQ ensures to send a personalized in-app message to this group as soon as they open the app, informing them about the upcoming 2 day discount sale.

Uninstalls Tracking

Post installation, a number of users end up uninstalling your application. Although the various app stores do provide you with that number, what about the root cause?

The reason can be anything; from fraudulent installations to an ill-timed push notification. rocQ derives specifics of your (ex) users & transforms them into meaningful patterns, even after they’ve uninstalled the app. This way you can optimize your app and reduce the chances of uninstallations in the future.

Benefits

  • Reason out app uninstalls & determine wider trends
  • Take action on insights, not assumptions
  • Discover worst performing campaigns

 

Example

You spend Rs. 40 per app install. But, do you know if the amount you spent on 1000 installs is actually getting you genuine users?

Out of 1000 installs, if 50% users uninstall your app, you’ve in fact spent Rs. 80 per install. rocQ tracks these numbers for you and enables you to invest your marketing budget wisely.
